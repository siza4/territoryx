TERMS OF SERVICE

1. Territory Ownership
TerritoryX operates a credit-based auction system. Winning a territory does not confer permanent ownership. Ownership is valid for the current epoch only and must be renewed via bidding or payment of the epoch invoice.

2. Credit & Payments
Credit limits are issued based on company profile and revenue estimates. All invoices must be paid within 30 days. Late payments incur a 1.5% fee and may result in account suspension after 14 days.

3. Billboard Content
Uploaded billboards must not contain illegal, offensive, or copyrighted material without license. TerritoryX reserves the right to remove non-compliant content and forfeit the territory.

4. Platform Toll
A 5% platform toll is applied to all winning bids. This is non-negotiable and included in the generated invoice.

5. Disputes
All bidding disputes are resolved by the TerritoryX arbitration panel. Admin decisions are final.

PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Collection
We collect your email, company profile, bidding history, and approximate IP-based location. We do not store precise GPS coordinates.

2. Use of Data
Your data is used for credit assessment, territory allocation, invoicing, and platform analytics. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Security
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access is controlled via Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced by Supabase.

4. Retention
Account data is retained for 7 years per tax regulations. You may request deletion by contacting finance@territoryx.africa.
